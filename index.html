The Man Behind the Metal

Three Decades of Metal Mastery

Reynaldo Meléndez learned the sheet metal trade in Mexico from master metalworkers, then brought those skills to Los Angeles. In 2013 he opened Rey's Sheet Metal and built a reputation the old way: exceptional quality, fair prices, and pieces that fit right the first time.

Today he serves contractors, builders, and homeowners across LA — from a single dryer vent to full commercial duct runs — with the same attention on every job. And every conversation can happen in English or Spanish.
